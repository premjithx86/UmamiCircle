# Specification: Content Engagement UI

## Overview
This track focuses on the user-facing pages for individual posts and recipes, and the interactive engagement components such as liking, commenting, and bookmarking.

## Objectives
1. Implement a detailed view for Posts and Recipes.
2. Build the interactive Engagement Bar (Like, Comment, Share, Bookmark).
3. Implement the Commenting system UI.

## Functional Requirements
- **Post Detail Page:**
    - Full-size image display.
    - Caption, tags, and user attribution.
    - Engagement bar.
- **Recipe Detail Page:**
    - Title, description, servings, time, and difficulty.
    - Ingredients list and step-by-step instructions.
    - SEO-optimized layout (JSON-LD ready).
- **Engagement Bar:**
    - Like toggle with animation.
    - Share button (link generation).
    - Bookmark toggle.
- **Comments System:**
    - Comment list with user info and timestamps.
    - Add/Delete comment functionality.
    - Basic sentiment/offensive text moderation on the frontend.

## Success Criteria
- Individual post and recipe URLs resolve correctly.
- Recipes are presented clearly with ingredients and steps.
- Likes and Bookmarks reflect in real-time.
- Comments are threaded or listed correctly and manageable by the owner.
