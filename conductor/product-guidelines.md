# UmamiCircle - Product Guidelines

## Brand Voice & Style
- **Authentic & Relatable:** We value real experiences and honest food reviews.
- **Vibrant & Visually-Driven:** High-quality food imagery is central to our platform.
- **Inspiriting & Educational:** We aim to spark culinary creativity and knowledge sharing.
- **Respectful & Safe:** Our community is a positive space, protected by AI moderation.

## UX Principles
- **Clarity & Simplicity:** Navigation and interactions should be intuitive and frictionless.
- **Responsive & Seamless:** Consistent user experience across devices.
- **Interactive & Real-time:** Immediate feedback and real-time connectivity (e.g., messaging).
- **Accessibility:** Ensuring everyone can enjoy and contribute to the platform.

## Design Guidelines
- **Modern & Clean Aesthetics:** Focus on the food content with minimal, effective design.
- **Consistent Visual Language:** Unified colors, typography, and iconography.
- **Brand Colors:**
    - Primary: `rgb(255, 153, 51)` (Orange)
    - Secondary: `rgb(255, 255, 255)` (White)
- **Dark Mode Support:** Mandatory across all pages.
- **URL Structure:**
    - Posts: `/posts/:slug`
    - Recipes: `/recipes/:slug`
    - Profiles: `/u/:username`
- **SEO Optimization:** Mandatory implementation of:
    - Standard Meta Tags
    - Open Graph
    - JSON-LD Structured Data for recipes.

## Content Standards
- **Authenticity First:** Encouraging original recipes and honest feedback.
- **Informative Content:** Recipes should be detailed and easy to follow.
- **Safety First:** Strict AI-driven moderation of offensive or inappropriate content.
- **Quality Over Quantity:** Promoting high-standard food imagery and recipes.
